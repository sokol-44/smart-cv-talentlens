# Rationale

## Why the Smart CV / TalentLens Project Was Created

### Problem Background

Between 2024 and 2026, recruitment processes underwent a significant transformation. The rapid growth of artificial intelligence tools, Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), and automated document analysis made creating an effective CV far more difficult than ever before.

In practice, candidates must simultaneously satisfy three often conflicting requirements:

1. **ATS and AI Requirements**
   - A CV must contain the right keywords.
   - The document must be optimized for automated filtering.
   - Candidates are evaluated by algorithms before a human ever sees their application.

2. **HR Recruiter Requirements**
   - The document should be concise, clear, and easy to scan.
   - Recruiters often spend less than a minute on an initial review.
   - Overly detailed CVs are frequently overlooked.

3. **Technical Leader and Manager Requirements**
   - They need detailed information about experience and expertise.
   - They expect business, architectural, and technical context.
   - They want to quickly identify relevant technologies, competencies, and project history.

The core issue is that a single CV document cannot effectively satisfy all of these needs at the same time.

As a result, candidates often make unfavorable compromises:

- Simplifying their CVs for ATS systems.
- Shortening project descriptions.
- Removing technical details.
- Omitting information about architecture and solution scale.

Such documents may successfully pass the initial screening stage but lose significant value when evaluated by technical decision-makers responsible for final hiring decisions.

### Observations That Led to the Project

The inspiration for the platform came from real-world experience gained through numerous recruitment processes, both as a candidate and as a professional working closely with technical teams.

Several recurring issues became apparent:

- HR departments and technical teams often evaluate candidates using completely different criteria.
- Critical specialist competencies are frequently omitted or underemphasized in job descriptions.
- Candidates have no practical way to present their experience at multiple levels of detail.
- Years of valuable project knowledge are compressed into a handful of short bullet points.
- Technical leaders waste time manually searching through documents for relevant information.

At the same time, advances in AI technology made it possible to create interfaces capable of presenting the same data in different contexts and formats tailored to different audiences.

### Project Goal

The goal of Smart CV / TalentLens is to separate professional experience data from the way that data is presented.

Instead of forcing candidates to create a single CV that attempts to satisfy every stakeholder, the platform enables the same information to be displayed through multiple independent views tailored to different audiences.

### Core Solution Principles

#### Simplified View

Designed for:

- HR departments
- Recruiters
- Initial screening teams

Characteristics:

- Clean and intuitive presentation of experience
- Focus on readability
- Summary of key competencies
- Rapid keyword discovery
- Visually appealing layout

#### Expert View

Designed for:

- Team Leaders
- Software Architects
- CTOs
- Engineering Managers

Characteristics:

- Complete project history
- Detailed technical information
- Advanced technology filtering
- Competency search capabilities
- Experience analysis by domain and specialization
- Fast access to projects matching specific requirements

### Use of Artificial Intelligence

The platform leverages modern language models to support:

- CV analysis
- Competency identification
- Experience categorization
- Automated summary generation
- Skill-to-job matching assistance
- Intelligent search and filtering mechanisms

A fundamental principle of the system is maintaining full user control over presented information while avoiding the generation of fictional or unsupported competencies.

### Business Value

The project delivers benefits to three primary stakeholder groups.

#### Candidates

- Can present their experience at the appropriate level of detail.
- No longer need to choose between ATS optimization and technical depth.
- Preserve the full context of their professional achievements.

#### Recruiters

- Can evaluate candidates more efficiently.
- Can identify role fit more quickly.
- Receive a consistent and structured overview of competencies.

#### Technical Leaders

- Can filter experience according to actual project requirements.
- Can quickly locate relevant implementations and achievements.
- Gain access to technical details without manually reviewing entire CVs.

### Why This Project Is Needed

Today's recruitment market suffers from a significant loss of information between candidates, HR departments, and technical teams.

Smart CV / TalentLens was created to bridge this gap by providing a platform that:

- Stores a complete professional experience history.
- Presents information differently depending on the audience.
- Enhances recruitment processes instead of oversimplifying them.
- Uses AI as an augmentation tool rather than a replacement for expert evaluation.

### Summary

Smart CV / TalentLens is a response to the growing disconnect between the requirements of ATS systems, HR departments, and technical hiring teams.

The project aims to enable multidimensional presentation of professional experience, preserve critical project context, and improve communication between all participants in the recruitment process.

Its core mission is to ensure that valuable professional expertise is not lost due to the limitations of traditional CV formats.
